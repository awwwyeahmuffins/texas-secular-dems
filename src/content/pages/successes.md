Since our founding in 2016, the Secular Democrats of Texas have organized, advocated, and won real victories inside the Texas Democratic Party and in our communities.

## Texas Democratic Party platform resolutions

We have successfully passed **three resolutions** into the Texas Democratic Party platform:

- **Resolution to Protect Children**
- **Resolution to Uphold Religious Freedom and the Separation of Church and State**
- **Resolution to Support Religiously Neutral Reproductive Healthcare**

## A growing presence at convention

At the **2018 Democratic State Convention in Fort Worth**, our caucus meeting drew a standing-room-only crowd — a clear sign of how quickly support for secular values is growing within the party.

## Standing up against conversion therapy

On **December 7, 2019**, we held a demonstration in **Addison, Texas** opposing conversion therapy. We continue to push for bans on this harmful practice at both the state and local levels of government.
